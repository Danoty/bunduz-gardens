About Bunduz Bondo Gardens

Bunduz Bondo Gardens is a luxury-class outdoor garden in Bondo, Kenya, open 24 hours a day. We offer a peaceful, secure and beautifully landscaped environment that is perfect for:

  • Relaxation and quiet personal time
  • Romantic dates and night chill spots
  • Professional and phone photoshoots
  • Picnics with friends and family
  • Small private events and celebrations

Step away from noise and stress. Enjoy fresh air, beautiful views and a calm atmosphere in the heart of Bondo.
